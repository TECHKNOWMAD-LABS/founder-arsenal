# SECTION A: DEFENCE & AEROSPACE

---

## 1. iDEX (Innovations for Defence Excellence)
- **Full Name:** Innovations for Defence Excellence
- **Department/Ministry:** Ministry of Defence, via Defence Innovation Organisation (DIO)
- **Funding Amount:** Grants up to INR 1.5 Cr per challenge (DISC); up to INR 10 Cr (DISC PRIME); up to INR 25 Cr (ADITI)
- **Overall Outlay:** INR 498.78 Cr (2021-22 to 2025-26); FY 2025-26 outlay: INR 449.62 Cr
- **Eligibility:** DPIIT-recognized startups, MSMEs, individual innovators
- **Application Method:** Apply via [idex.gov.in](https://idex.gov.in/challenges) against specific problem statements
- **Status:** ACTIVE. DISC 14 (82 problem statements) and ADITI 4th edition both open, deadline 04 May 2026. 549 problem statements issued to date, 619 startups engaged, 430 contracts awarded. Procurement orders >INR 2,400 Cr placed for 43 iDEX products.

### 1a. DISC (Defence India Startup Challenges)
- **Current Edition:** DISC 14
- **Funding:** Up to INR 1.5 Cr (standard); up to INR 10 Cr (PRIME)
- **Status:** ACTIVE, deadline 04 May 2026

### 1b. ADITI (Acing Development of Innovative Technologies with iDEX)
- **Current Edition:** ADITI 4.0
- **Corpus:** INR 750 Cr (FY 2023-24 to 2025-26) for 30 deep-tech projects
- **Funding:** Up to INR 25 Cr per project
- **Focus Areas:** AI, quantum, cyber-tech, satellite communication, semiconductors, autonomous weapons, underwater surveillance
- **Status:** ACTIVE, deadline 04 May 2026

---

## 2. DRDO TDF (Technology Development Fund)
- **Full Name:** Technology Development Fund Scheme
- **Department/Ministry:** Ministry of Defence, executed by DRDO
- **Funding Amount:** Up to 90% of project cost, capped at INR 50 Cr (Grant-in-Aid). Separate INR 500 Cr corpus for deep-tech vertical.
- **Eligibility:** Indian startups, MSMEs, academic institutions developing indigenous defence and dual-use technologies
- **Application Method:** Submit proposals via [tdf.drdo.gov.in](https://tdf.drdo.gov.in/) against published RFPs
- **Status:** ACTIVE. Between Jan 2023 and June 2024, INR 120 Cr allocated; 16 MSMEs and 20 startups benefited. Targets TRL 3+ technologies.

---

## 3. DRDO DARE to DREAM
- **Full Name:** Dare to Dream Innovation Contest
- **Department/Ministry:** DRDO, Ministry of Defence
- **Funding Amount:** Up to INR 10 lakh for startups; up to INR 5 lakh for individuals
- **Eligibility:** Indian citizens (18+), DPIIT-recognized startups, Micro & Small Enterprises registered with Ministry of MSME
- **Application Method:** Via DRDO website ([drdo.gov.in](https://drdo.gov.in/drdo/en/announcement/dare-dream-50-innovation-contest))
- **Status:** Dare to Dream 5.0 conducted in 2024-25. Watch for 6.0 announcement. Focus: disruptive ideas in defence & aerospace emerging technologies.
