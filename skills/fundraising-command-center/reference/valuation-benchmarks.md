## Deal Structure Benchmarks

### SAFE Market Share (2025)
- Pre-Seed: 90% SAFE, 10% other
- Seed: 64% SAFE, 27% priced equity, 10% convertible note
- Post-Money SAFEs: 85% of all SAFEs signed
- Cap-only SAFEs: 61% of all SAFEs

### Term Sheet Standards (Cooley Q2 2025, 238 financings)
- 1x liquidation preference: 98% of deals
- Non-participating preferred: 95% of deals
- Investor veto rights: 90%+ of deals
- Pay-to-play: ~10% (rising)
- Anti-dilution protections: 46% of deals

### Dilution Trajectory (Cumulative)

## Timeline Benchmarks
- Seed to Series A: 774 days / 2.1 years (Q4 2024, up from 420 days in 2021)
- Active fundraising process: ~6 months average
- Pitches per term sheet: 20-30
- Warm intro speed advantage: 40% faster close
- Bridge round share of capital: 16.6% (Q2 2025)
- Down rounds: 15.9% of deals (2025, decade high)
