## 1. MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (DPIIT)

### 1.1 Startup India Recognition
- **Full Name:** DPIIT Startup Recognition Programme
- **Ministry/Dept:** Department for Promotion of Industry & Internal Trade (DPIIT)
- **Funding:** No direct funding; provides tax exemptions, regulatory benefits
- **Eligibility:** Private Ltd Co / LLP / Partnership; <10 years old (20 years for deep tech); turnover <Rs 200 Cr (Rs 300 Cr for deep tech)
- **Status:** ACTIVE (1,97,692+ startups recognized as of Oct 2025)
- **Website:** https://www.startupindia.gov.in/

### 1.2 Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS)
- **Full Name:** Startup India Seed Fund Scheme
- **Ministry/Dept:** DPIIT
- **Funding:** Up to Rs 50 lakh per startup (through incubators); Total corpus Rs 945 Cr
- **Eligibility:** DPIIT-recognized; <2 years old; tech-based; not received >Rs 10 lakh from other govt schemes
- **Status:** ACTIVE
- **Website:** https://seedfund.startupindia.gov.in/

### 1.3 Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS)
- **Full Name:** Fund of Funds for Startups
- **Ministry/Dept:** DPIIT (managed by SIDBI)
- **Funding:** Rs 10,000 Cr corpus; Rs 21,276 Cr invested in 1,173 startups (as of Dec 2024)
- **Eligibility:** Indirect - through SEBI-registered AIFs/VCs
- **Status:** ACTIVE
- **Website:** https://www.startupindia.gov.in/

### 1.4 Credit Guarantee Scheme for Startups (CGSS)
- **Full Name:** Credit Guarantee Scheme for Startups
- **Ministry/Dept:** DPIIT
- **Funding:** Guarantee cover on loans Rs 10 lakh to Rs 10 Cr from banks/NBFCs/VDFs
- **Eligibility:** DPIIT-recognized startups
- **Status:** ACTIVE
- **Website:** https://www.startupindia.gov.in/

### 1.5 Startup India IP Protection (SIPP)
- **Full Name:** Scheme for Facilitating Startups Intellectual Property Protection
- **Ministry/Dept:** DPIIT
- **Funding:** Govt bears entire facilitator fees; 80% rebate on patent filing fees
- **Eligibility:** DPIIT-recognized startups
- **Status:** ACTIVE
- **Website:** https://www.startupindia.gov.in/

### 1.6 Angel Tax Abolition
- **Full Name:** Section 56(2)(viib) Exemption
- **Ministry/Dept:** DPIIT / Ministry of Finance
- **Funding:** Tax exemption - no tax on angel investments (effective FY 2025-26)
- **Eligibility:** All startups raising capital
- **Status:** ACTIVE (from FY 2025-26)

### 1.7 Income Tax Exemption (Section 80-IAC)
- **Full Name:** Tax Holiday for Startups
- **Ministry/Dept:** DPIIT / CBDT
- **Funding:** 100% income tax exemption for 3 consecutive years out of first 10 years
- **Eligibility:** DPIIT-recognized; incorporated after 01.04.2016
- **Status:** ACTIVE

## 2. MINISTRY OF MSME

### 2.1 MSME Champions Scheme (Umbrella)
- **Full Name:** MSME Innovative (Champions) Scheme
- **Ministry/Dept:** Ministry of MSME
- **Funding:** Umbrella scheme merging 6 schemes; various components
- **Eligibility:** Registered MSMEs (Udyam registered)
- **Status:** ACTIVE (2021-22 to 2025-26)
- **Website:** https://innovative.msme.gov.in/

### 2.2 Credit Linked Capital Subsidy Scheme (CLCSS)
- **Full Name:** Credit Linked Capital Subsidy - Technology Upgradation Scheme (CLCS-TUS)
- **Ministry/Dept:** Ministry of MSME
- **Funding:** 15% capital subsidy on institutional finance up to Rs 1 Cr for technology upgradation
- **Eligibility:** MSMEs (micro & small enterprises); registered under Udyam
- **Status:** ACTIVE (CLCSS 2.0 under consideration)
- **Website:** https://msme.gov.in/

### 2.3 Zero Defect Zero Effect (ZED) Certification
- **Full Name:** ZED Certification Scheme
- **Ministry/Dept:** Ministry of MSME
- **Funding:** Financial assistance for ZED certification; preference in govt procurement; subsidized loans
- **Eligibility:** All MSMEs
- **Status:** ACTIVE (target: 2 lakh+ MSMEs certified in FY 2025-26)
- **Website:** https://zed.msme.gov.in/

### 2.4 SFURTI (Traditional Industries)
- **Full Name:** Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries
- **Ministry/Dept:** Ministry of MSME
- **Funding:** Up to Rs 2.5 Cr for regular clusters (500 artisans); Rs 5 Cr for major clusters (1000+ artisans)
- **Eligibility:** Clusters of traditional industry artisans (khadi, coir, bamboo, agarbatti, etc.)
- **Status:** ACTIVE (513 clusters approved; 376 functional)
- **Website:** https://sfurti.msme.gov.in/

### 2.5 ASPIRE (Innovation & Rural Entrepreneurship)
- **Full Name:** A Scheme for Promotion of Innovation, Rural Industries and Entrepreneurship
- **Ministry/Dept:** Ministry of MSME
- **Funding:** Rs 30-100 lakh grants for agro-based and rural startups; fund of funds for early-stage
- **Eligibility:** Rural entrepreneurs; agri-based startups
- **Status:** ACTIVE
- **Website:** https://msme.gov.in/

### 2.6 MSME Incubation (under Champions)
- **Full Name:** MSME Innovative - Incubation Component
- **Ministry/Dept:** Ministry of MSME
- **Funding:** Up to Rs 1 Cr per idea; up to Rs 15 lakh per incubatee
- **Eligibility:** Startups incubated at MSME-supported incubators
- **Status:** ACTIVE
- **Website:** https://innovative.msme.gov.in/

### 2.7 IP Facilitation for MSMEs
- **Full Name:** MSME Innovative - IPR Component / IP Facilitation Centres
- **Ministry/Dept:** Ministry of MSME
- **Funding:** Reimbursement: up to Rs 5 lakh (foreign patent), Rs 1 lakh (domestic patent), Rs 2 lakh (GI), Rs 15,000 (design), Rs 10,000 (trademark)
- **Eligibility:** All MSMEs through IPFCs
- **Status:** ACTIVE
- **Website:** https://innovative.msme.gov.in/

### 2.8 Digital MSME
- **Full Name:** Digital MSME Component (under CLCS-TUS)
- **Ministry/Dept:** Ministry of MSME
- **Funding:** Cloud computing/ICT adoption support; target 1 million MSMEs by 2026
- **Eligibility:** All MSMEs
- **Status:** ACTIVE

### 2.9 Design Component (under Champions)
- **Full Name:** MSME Innovative - Design Component
- **Ministry/Dept:** Ministry of MSME
- **Funding:** Up to Rs 40 lakh per design project; Rs 2.5 lakh per student design project
- **Eligibility:** MSMEs seeking design improvement
- **Status:** ACTIVE

### 2.10 TReDS (Trade Receivables Discounting System)
- **Full Name:** Trade Receivables Discounting System
- **Ministry/Dept:** Ministry of MSME / RBI
- **Funding:** Invoice discounting platform; no direct grant but enables faster cash flow
- **Eligibility:** MSMEs with receivables from corporates/govt
- **Status:** ACTIVE (mandatory for large buyers to register)
- **Website:** https://msme.gov.in/

### 2.11 MSME SAMBANDH (Procurement Policy)
- **Full Name:** Public Procurement Policy for MSMEs / MSME SAMBANDH Portal
- **Ministry/Dept:** Ministry of MSME
- **Funding:** 25% of total procurement from MSMEs mandated for CPSEs (4% from SC/ST MSMEs, 3% from women MSMEs)
- **Eligibility:** All registered MSMEs
- **Status:** ACTIVE
- **Website:** https://sambandh.msme.gov.in/

### 2.12 Lean Manufacturing Competitiveness (under Champions)
- **Full Name:** Lean Manufacturing Competitiveness Scheme
- **Ministry/Dept:** Ministry of MSME
- **Funding:** 80% govt contribution for lean consulting
- **Eligibility:** MSMEs in clusters
- **Status:** ACTIVE

### 2.13 Micro & Small Enterprises - Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP)
- **Full Name:** MSE-CDP
- **Ministry/Dept:** Ministry of MSME
- **Funding:** Up to Rs 10 Cr for Common Facility Centres; Rs 5 Cr for infrastructure development
- **Eligibility:** MSME clusters
- **Status:** ACTIVE

- **Ministry/Dept:** Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers' Welfare
- **Funding:** Up to Rs 25 lakh for startups with MVP; seed funding through R-ABIs
- **Eligibility:** Agri-tech startups; innovators in agriculture and allied sectors
- **Status:** ACTIVE

### 3.2 ACABC (Agri Clinics & Agri Business Centres)
- **Full Name:** Agri-Clinics and Agri-Business Centres Scheme
- **Ministry/Dept:** Ministry of Agriculture / NABARD / MANAGE
- **Funding:** Loans up to Rs 20 lakh (Rs 25 lakh in special cases); Rs 1 Cr for groups; 36-44% subsidy
- **Eligibility:** Agriculture graduates (including diploma holders) after 2-month training
- **Status:** ACTIVE
- **Website:** https://www.agriclinics.net/

### 3.3 Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF)
- **Full Name:** Agriculture Infrastructure Fund
- **Ministry/Dept:** Ministry of Agriculture
- **Funding:** Rs 1 lakh Cr fund; 3% interest subvention on loans up to Rs 2 Cr; credit guarantee through CGTMSE
- **Eligibility:** Farmers, FPOs, PACS, cooperatives, startups, agri-entrepreneurs
- **Status:** ACTIVE (Rs 66,310 Cr sanctioned for 1,13,419 projects as of June 2025; disbursement till FY 2025-26)
- **Website:** https://agriinfra.dac.gov.in/

### 3.4 Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanisation (SMAM)
- **Full Name:** Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanisation
- **Ministry/Dept:** Ministry of Agriculture
- **Funding:** 40-100% subsidy on farm machinery; CHC establishment support
- **Eligibility:** Farmers, FPOs, Custom Hiring Centres
- **Status:** ACTIVE

### 3.5 MIDH (Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture)
- **Full Name:** Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture
- **Ministry/Dept:** Ministry of Agriculture
- **Funding:** Credit-linked back-ended subsidy (35-50%) for cold storage, pack houses, food processing
- **Eligibility:** Farmers, entrepreneurs, FPOs, companies in horticulture
- **Status:** ACTIVE
- **Website:** https://midh.gov.in/

### 3.6 e-NAM (National Agriculture Market)
- **Full Name:** Electronic National Agriculture Market
- **Ministry/Dept:** Ministry of Agriculture
- **Funding:** Rs 75 lakh per mandi for infrastructure upgradation; provides market access platform
- **Eligibility:** Farmers, traders, FPOs, agri-tech platforms
- **Status:** ACTIVE (1,361 mandis integrated)
- **Website:** https://enam.gov.in/

## 4. MINISTRY OF FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (MoFPI)

### 4.1 PMKSY (Pradhan Mantri Kisan SAMPADA Yojana)
- **Full Name:** Pradhan Mantri Kisan SAMPADA Yojana
- **Ministry/Dept:** MoFPI
- **Funding:** Total outlay Rs 6,520 Cr (15th Finance Commission cycle); grants for cold chain, agro-processing clusters, food processing units
- **Eligibility:** Entrepreneurs, companies, FPOs, cooperatives in food processing
- **Status:** ACTIVE (till 2025-26); 1,608 projects sanctioned
- **Website:** https://www.mofpi.gov.in/

### 4.2 PMFME (PM Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises)
- **Full Name:** Pradhan Mantri Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises
- **Ministry/Dept:** MoFPI
- **Funding:** Rs 10,000 Cr outlay (2020-21 to 2025-26); credit-linked subsidy of 35% of project cost (max Rs 10 lakh) for individual units
- **Eligibility:** Existing micro food processing enterprises; SHGs; FPOs
- **Status:** ACTIVE (1,62,744 loans sanctioned till Oct 2025)
- **Website:** https://pmfme.mofpi.gov.in/

### 4.3 PLI for Food Processing Industry (PLISFPI)
- **Full Name:** Production Linked Incentive Scheme for Food Processing Industry
- **Ministry/Dept:** MoFPI
- **Funding:** Rs 10,900 Cr outlay (2021-22 to 2026-27); incentive 4-10% of incremental sales
- **Eligibility:** Large food processing companies (Category I: sales >Rs 250 Cr), SME/innovative (Category II: Rs 1-250 Cr sales), organic product applicants
- **Status:** ACTIVE (170 applications approved)

### 4.4 Operation Greens (TOP to TOTAL)
- **Full Name:** Operation Greens - Tomato, Onion, Potato to TOTAL (all fruits & vegetables)
- **Ministry/Dept:** MoFPI
- **Funding:** 50% subsidy on transportation and storage of eligible crops
- **Eligibility:** FPOs, cooperatives, food processors
- **Status:** ACTIVE (44 projects sanctioned under PMKSY-OG)

### 4.5 Mega Food Parks (Legacy)
- **Full Name:** Mega Food Parks Scheme
- **Ministry/Dept:** MoFPI
- **Funding:** Up to Rs 50 Cr per Mega Food Park
- **Eligibility:** SPVs setting up food parks with central processing centres
- **Status:** LEGACY (41 sanctioned; new sanctions discontinued; existing commitments honoured)

## 34. CROSS-MINISTRY / NITI AAYOG / ATAL INNOVATION MISSION

### 34.1 Atal Innovation Mission (AIM)
- **Full Name:** Atal Innovation Mission
- **Ministry/Dept:** NITI Aayog
- **Funding:** Rs 2,000 Cr budget; supports ATLs, AICs, AMICs
- **Eligibility:** Schools (ATLs), incubators (AICs), corporates (AMICs)
- **Status:** ACTIVE
- **Website:** https://aim.gov.in/

### 34.2 Atal Incubation Centres (AICs)
- **Full Name:** Atal Incubation Centres
- **Ministry/Dept:** NITI Aayog / AIM
- **Funding:** Up to Rs 10 Cr grant-in-aid per AIC over 5 years
- **Eligibility:** Academic institutions, corporates, NGOs setting up incubators
- **Status:** ACTIVE (68+ AICs established)

### 34.3 Atal Tinkering Labs (ATLs)
- **Full Name:** Atal Tinkering Labs
- **Ministry/Dept:** NITI Aayog / AIM
- **Funding:** Rs 20 lakh per lab (Rs 12 lakh grant + Rs 8 lakh from school)
- **Eligibility:** Schools across India
- **Status:** ACTIVE (10,000+ ATLs)

### 34.4 Mudra Yojana (PMMY)
- **Full Name:** Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana
- **Ministry/Dept:** Ministry of Finance / MUDRA
- **Funding:** Loans without collateral: Shishu (up to Rs 50,000), Kishor (Rs 50,000-5 lakh), Tarun (Rs 5-20 lakh)
- **Eligibility:** Any Indian citizen for non-farm income-generating activity; micro/small enterprises
- **Status:** ACTIVE (Rs 30.5 lakh Cr disbursed to 50 Cr+ accounts)
- **Website:** https://www.mudra.org.in/

### 34.5 National SC-ST Hub
- **Full Name:** National SC-ST Hub
- **Ministry/Dept:** Ministry of MSME / NSIC
- **Funding:** Capacity building; handholding; procurement facilitation for SC/ST entrepreneurs
- **Eligibility:** SC/ST MSMEs
- **Status:** ACTIVE
- **Website:** https://www.scsthub.in/

### 34.6 CGTMSE (Credit Guarantee for MSMEs)
- **Full Name:** Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises
- **Ministry/Dept:** Ministry of MSME / SIDBI
- **Funding:** Collateral-free credit guarantee up to Rs 5 Cr; enhanced to Rs 10 Cr for select MSMEs
- **Eligibility:** Micro and small enterprises (manufacturing + services)
- **Status:** ACTIVE
- **Website:** https://www.cgtmse.in/

### 34.7 SIDBI Fund of Funds / Venture Capital
- **Full Name:** SIDBI Make in India Soft Loan Fund (SMILE) + various funds
- **Ministry/Dept:** SIDBI
- **Funding:** Soft loans, quasi-equity; various windows for MSMEs and startups
- **Eligibility:** MSMEs, startups, aspiring entrepreneurs
- **Status:** ACTIVE
- **Website:** https://www.sidbi.in/

### 34.8 Bharat Startup Grand Challenge
- **Full Name:** Bharat Startup Grand Challenge
- **Ministry/Dept:** Various ministries through Startup India
- **Funding:** Problem-statement challenges from multiple ministries; prizes + adoption support
- **Eligibility:** DPIIT-recognized startups
- **Status:** ACTIVE
- **Website:** https://www.startupindia.gov.in/content/sih/en/bharat-startup-grand-challenge.html
